A little bit about me

Hello! My name is Gini Guerinot. I'm a mom of five who loves spending time in the kitchen. My love for cooking stems from my French roots and love for food in general. I love cooking and baking and sharing my creations with the people I love. I also have a love for teaching. I am a culinary arts teacher at the local high school which inspired me to start teaching classes out of my own home! I offer cooking classes for adults and kids and have a lot of fun doing it!

I have a teaching degree and have taught both at the college level and the highschool loevel. I taught English to French students, French to American high school students and Culinary Arts at the highschool level as well.

I have such a deap ove for cooking and teaching others how to cook. I love helping both kids and adult learn how to nourish themselves in sustainable ways. I love helping kids and adults discover new foods and creative ways to incorporate all the necessary elements for a healthy diet.

I teach from all expereince levels and host classes for a selection of age groups. If you or your kids are interested in learnin basic cooking skills, exploring new recipes, and learning how to prepare easy and delicious meals, sign up for a class!
